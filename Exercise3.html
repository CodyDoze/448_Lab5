Big Jay

Big Jay is the official mascot of the Kansas Jayhawks. Big Jay became the mascot for the Jayhawks in the 1960s. You can find Big Jay at almost any KU sporting event with his friend Baby Jay who was hatched from an egg at halftime of a football game in 1971.
